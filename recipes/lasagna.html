Lasagna


Description

Lasagna is a layered Italian dish made with wide pasta sheets, tomato sauce, meat, and cheese, baked until bubbly and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Wide pasta sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground meat
  • Cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Onion and garlic

Steps

  1. Cook pasta until al dente, then drain.
  2. Brown ground meat with onions, garlic, and seasonings; add tomato sauce.
  3. Mix ricotta, Parmesan, and parsley for cheese mixture.
  4. Layer: sauce, pasta, cheese mixture, mozzarella; repeat.
  5. Bake covered, then uncovered until golden and bubbly.
  6. Rest briefly, slice, and serve hot. Enjoy!
