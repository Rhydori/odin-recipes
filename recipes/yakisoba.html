Yakisoba


Description

Yakisoba is a Japanese stir-fried noodle dish with veggies, protein, and savory sauce. It's a flavorful and satisfying street food favorite.

Ingredients

  • Yakisoba noodles
  • Assorted vegetables (cabbage, carrots, bell peppers, onions)
  • Protein (chicken, pork, beef, tofu)
  • Yakisoba sauce
  • Oil (vegetable or sesame)
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil Yakisoba noodles until al dente; drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add protein and cook until browned and cooked through.
  4. Add assorted vegetables and stir-fry until tender.
  5. Move ingredients to the side of the skillet; add a little more oil if needed.
  6. Add cooked noodles to the skillet and pour Yakisoba sauce over them.
  7. Toss everything together until noodles are evenly coated with sauce.
  8. Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper to taste.
  9. Cook for a few more minutes, stirring occasionally, until heated through.
  10. Optional: Garnish with chopped green onions, sesame seeds, or bonito flakes before serving.
  11. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious homemade Yakisoba!
