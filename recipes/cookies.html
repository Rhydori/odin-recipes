Cookies


Description

A cookie is a small, sweet baked treat made from flour, sugar, butter, and flavorings, like chocolate chips or nuts, enjoyed as a snack or dessert.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Baking powder
  • Chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175Â°C.
  2. Cream together butter and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract.
  4. Mix in flour and baking powder until well combined.
  5. Optionally, fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until edges are lightly golden.
  8. Let cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
  9. Enjoy your homemade cookies!
